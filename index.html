Notes:

– Based on data from Corona API. Different countries count cases and test populations differently.
– Chance of having COVID19 = (Conf.C. × multiplicator of Undoc.C. − Rec. − Deaths) / Popul. × 100.
– Default hospitalization and fatality rates based on multiple sources, The Covid-19 risks for different age groups, explained, Coronavirus disease 2019.
– Vehicle crash probability based on Global Status Report on Road Safety 2018.
– We are not epidemiologists or medical scientists.
