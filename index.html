You have 0.2109 % chance to get infected.

You have 0.0421 % chance to need hospitalization (+ICU).

You have 0.0071 % chance that you will die.

The chance that you will die this year in a vehicle crash is 0.0182 %.

If you need hospitalization, there is a chance that you will end with long-term health problems.

If you are young and need hospitalization, there is a small chance that someone else will die because of triage.

Notes:
– The model does not count on transmission risk, gender, you wearing a mask, passing people on the street or touching infected surfaces.
– We use data from pomber / covid19 and samayo / country-json. Different countries count cases and test populations differently.
– Chance of having COVID19 = (Conf.C. × multiplicator of Undoc.C. − Rec. − Deaths) / Popul. × 100.
– Default hospitalization and fatality rates based on multiple sources, e.g. The Covid-19 risks for different age groups, explained, Coronavirus disease 2019.
– Vehicle crash probability based on Global Status Report on Road Safety 2018.
– We are not epidemiologists or medical scientists.
